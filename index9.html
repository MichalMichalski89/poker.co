Services

You will get: the poker surfaces, the chips, the poker decks, the buttons and other bits and pieces you need to run your a legal game at your pub without any special license.

We will provide you with access to your online tools to keep and publish the results of your games.

If you decide to run weekly league games, you will be able to award a number of tickets to our regional finals to your best players, as well as a special invite to an exclusive tournament directors' event with a guaranteed cash prizes.

We will be there to offer to support you in order to make your events succesfull. We will advertise your games through a variety of means including a unique entries on our Pub Poker Map and physical adverising pieces such as leaflets, posters or coastes.

We can train your staff in setting up and/or running the game, or help you find local players who could take charge of games on your behalf, often at no extra charge.
